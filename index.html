Mirador demonstration

This site provides a very simple demonstration of the Mirador IIIF viewer to present page image scans of works by Alexander Pope held by the British Library and Harvard University.
The site consists of two pages:
  • A comparison of an authorised London edition of one of Pope's Horatian imitations alongside an unauthorised Edinburgh edition of the same title.
  • A juxtaposition of the manuscript of Pope's Ethic Epistles held by Harvard University with the text of a copy of the first edition held by the British Library.
